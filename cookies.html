Cookie Policy

This page explains how InventoryOS uses cookies, local storage and similar technologies on the public website and app.

On this page Summary What we use Why we use it Your choices Updates

1. Summary

InventoryOS uses cookies, browser storage and similar technologies to make the website and app work. We do not currently use advertising cookies on the public website.

2. What we use

Essential cookies/storageUsed for login, security, sessions, plan flow and keeping the app working.
Local storageUsed for app preferences such as selected inventory, selected workspace, theme, API base and login token storage.
StripeStripe Checkout and billing pages may use cookies or similar technologies for payment, fraud prevention and checkout security.
GoogleGoogle OAuth may use Google cookies when you choose to connect Gmail.
AnalyticsIf analytics are added later, this page should be updated and consent collected where required.

3. Why cookies and storage are used

  • To keep you logged in and protect your session.
  • To remember workspace, inventory and theme choices.
  • To support billing, checkout and fraud-prevention flows.
  • To support optional integrations such as Google OAuth.

4. Your choices

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. Some essential parts of InventoryOS may not work correctly if you disable cookies or browser storage, including login, billing and workspace selection.

Where non-essential cookies are introduced, InventoryOS should provide a clear choice before setting them.

5. Updates

This policy should be updated if InventoryOS adds analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, embedded third-party tools or other non-essential storage technologies.
